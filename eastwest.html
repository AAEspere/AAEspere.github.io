eastwesthealthservices.com

eastwesthealthservices.com is a website I created while working as a receptionist at EastWest Health Services. EastWest Health Services is a healthcare located in Sterling Heights. Orignally, the website was supposed to be created with WordPress, but I noticed that there were frustrations with the limitations of WordPress and creative design. I was tasked with learning HTML, and creating the website for the company.

The website uses an HTML/CSS/JavaScript front end, PHP backend, and just displays basic information about the company. This website mostly had a positive effect on the company. It increased activity in the company after publication, since the company did not have a website before, and didn't have an easy way to contact them online. I'm currently still working on the website, I have plans to integrate user management into the website: allowing workers to log in and upload their medical documents onto a database where the owners can access everything.
